Welcome to Duckhunt

Duckhunt is a fast paced shooting game where you team up with Disney's Donald Duck for a day of wild hunting and cartoon chaos. Your goal is simple; control the crosshair with your mouse movement and hit as many ducks as you can. Whatever you do, do not shoot Donald Duck, or you will face his hilarious and furious reaction. Do your best and compare you result with your friends after each game. The animations and energetic sound effects make every shot feel exciting and unpredictable.

Duckhunt captures the spirit of classic arcade fun with a modern twist that keeps you on your toes. It is a lighthearted test of precision, patience, and quick thinking. Whether you play for a challenge or for laughter, DuckHunt guarantees a quacking good time.

Duck

The regular duck glides across the screen from right to left, testing your aim and timing. When you hit it, the duck will fall and disappear, rewarding you with 1 point for your accuracy. Keep your eyes sharp, they may look easy, but they move faster than you think!

Super Duck

The super duck is rare and unpredictable, popping up at random places on the screen. Hit it successfully and it will implode, and let out a duck sound as you earn 2 points. It's your best chance to boost your score, but you will have to react quick!

Donald Duck

Your hunting partner Donald Duck appears unexpectedly in random places. Whatever you do, don't shoot him. If you hit Donald, he will implode in outrage and disappear while screaming at you. Aim carefully, because one mistake might cost you 2 points and Donald's patience!

Customize Your Hunt Before You Begin

Before starting the hunt, you can customize your game in the settings menu. Here you choose both the difficulty level and the background for your adventure. The difficulty determines how fast the ducks move and how challenging the gameplay becomes. Selecting Easy is great if you're new to the game or just want a relaxed experience, while Normal and Hard offer a faster and more intense challenge. You can also pick the scenery you want to play in, such as Space, Waterfall, or Beach, each giving the game a unique look and feel. If you like surprises, choose Random background and let the game decide for you. All settings are selected by simply clicking on the option you prefer. Once you're ready, press Continue to begin your hunt.

Phone compatible

You can enjoy the game on your cellphone. Because it is easier to play on the phone, the pattern of speed of the ducks are a bit different. The size of the ducks are also adjusted to increase the experience of the game. All the features are the same and the game display is adjusted to your screen size.

Tablet compatible

The game is also tablet compatible. The movement of the ducks are adjusted and so is the size. The features, animations and the sounds are same, so you can enjoy the game on the device of your choice.

Compete

After finishing a round, your score appears in the Highscore Table. The table shows your result alongside the difficulty you played on, so you can see how well you performed in that session. Each new score is added to the list only when you press the “Play again” button. If you exit the game, your scores won't be saved. This way, you can keep track of your progress during one play session and try to improve each round. Make sure you try harder difficulties, just trying a harder difficulty is ranked above easier difficulties. The highlighted row shows your latest result, making it easy to see how your performance compares to your previous attempts. Once you're ready, click Play again to start a new hunt and see if you can beat your current high score.

Effects

Intro

The game begins with a short animated intro that sets the tone for the adventure ahead. The animation introduces the hunting scene and gives a playful hint of what's to come. The visual creates excitement before the action begins. Press the "start game" button to start the game.

Timer

In the top right corner of the screen, you'll find the game's timer counting down every second. It adds intensity to each round, pushing you to shoot as many ducks as possible before time runs out. The faster you react, the higher your score will be. When the timer reaches zero, the hunt is over, and your final score is revealed.

Crosshair

The regular mouse pointer has been replaced with a gun crosshair, giving the game a more immersive and authentic shooting experience. It helps you aim precisely at the ducks and adds to the feeling of being on a real hunt. Every movement of the crosshair matters, accuracy is the key to a high score.

Sound effects

Every shot is followed by a satisfying gun sound, making each hit feel impactful. The ducks an Super Ducks respond with their own quacking sounds. And of course, if you accidentally shoot Donald Duck, you'll hear his unmistakable “No!”. The intro also has a classic sound effect. Hear all the sounds below.

Animations

The animations in Duckhunt make every moment of gameplay feel alive and dynamic. When you hit a regular duck, it falls from the sky, spinning as it goes, before it slowly fades away and disappears. This small detail adds a sense of motion and satisfaction to every successful shot. The super ducks and Donald Duck, on the other hand, behave differently when hit. Instead of falling, they rotate and implode. Together, these animations bring rhythm, humor, and visual excitement to every round of the hunt. See the animations in the video below.
